Reflective Pavements - Detailed Information

Explore the use of light-colored and reflective materials for roads and sidewalks to reduce heat absorption.

  • Types: High-albedo asphalt and concrete, coatings, permeable pavements.
  • Benefits: Lower surface temperatures, reduced radiant heat, improved nighttime temperatures.
  • Considerations: Durability, maintenance, cost-effectiveness.
