Green Roofs - Detailed Information

This page provides detailed information about the benefits, implementation, and examples of green roofs as a cooling intervention.

  • Benefits: Reduced heat island effect, insulation, stormwater management, increased biodiversity, aesthetics.
  • Implementation: Types of green roofs (extensive, intensive), layers involved (waterproofing, drainage, growing medium, vegetation), maintenance.
  • Examples: Case studies of successful green roof installations in urban areas.
